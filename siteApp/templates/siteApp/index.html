Intro

Andrew was educated at the University of Alberta in the Computer Engineering: Software Specialization program. During his education, he worked with world class companies such as Nokia and PCL Construction, and graduated in 2019. After graduation, he worked with leading AI and machine learning experts at Carbeeza, before finally striking out into the world of freelance work. For more detail on his work, check out his awesome portfolio, or the services he provides.

In his spare time, Andrew keeps busy by building video games as a fun and creative way to keep his coding skills sharp. He enjoys tabletop games of all kinds, and loves to volunteer his time with local community organizations, as well as events such as Extra Life.
